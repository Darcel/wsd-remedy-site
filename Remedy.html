Remedies For The Common Complaint!

Pain Be Gone With Coffee!

Studies show that caffeine motivates cells that block pain sensations about 10% to 20% more effective than OTC painkillers! For mild pain sip one 8oz cup and for more intense pain sip two 8oz cups.

Acne Prone? Try Yeast!

Yeast is loaded with a B vitamin called niacin, which reduces excessive oil production. When used topically it helps acne breakouts by 14% according to a study in the International Journal of Dermatology. Mix 1tsp. powdered brewer's yeast into 2tsp. plain yogurt. Spread on problem areas and leave on 10min before rinsing off. Repeat every three to seven days.

Rinse Away Dandruff With Green Tea!

Polyphenols in green tea cut down on flakes by slowing overproduction of skin cells. So before retiring to bed brew a cup of green tea, allow it to cool then massage it into your hair and scalp and leave it on overnight.Make sure you brew a fresh pot every use due to green tea losing its antioxidants after a few hours, suggests Stephen Hsu, Ph.D.
